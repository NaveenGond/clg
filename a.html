ADMISSION THROUGH CET

To seek admission into the various BTech courses in the state of Karnataka, candidates must qualify for any one of the KCET, COMEDK-UGET, or JEE Main entrance exams. The registration process for KCET 2023 will begin in the month of May 2023, and COMEDK UGET 2023 began on February 15, 2023.

ADMISSION THROUGH COMED-K

Candidates who wish to get admission to the participating institutes in COMEDK UGET 2023 have to fulfil the eligibility criteria and submit the application form. Moreover, the candidates have to qualify the COMEDK UGET 2023 entrance test and score above the cutoff marks to participate in the counseling process.

ADMISSION THROUGH MANAGEMENT

Management quota admission is a provision made by colleges and universities for students who want to secure admission in top colleges but have not been able to secure admission through the regular admission process.

ELIGIBILITY

Candidates must have passed 10+2/PUC from a well-recognised Central/ State government education board. Candidates should have passed Classes 11 and 12 with subjects Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.
